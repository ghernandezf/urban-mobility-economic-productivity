# 🚦 Urban Mobility & Economic Productivity Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

Urban mobility plays a critical role in economic development by influencing productivity, accessibility, and quality of life. This project analyzes the relationship between urban mobility indicators and economic performance across major Latin American cities.

Using real-world datasets from the **TomTom Traffic Index** and **OECD Cities**, the analysis explores whether traffic congestion and travel conditions are associated with GDP per capita and identifies insights that can support infrastructure investment decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

Evaluate how urban mobility impacts economic productivity in major Latin American cities by:

* Cleaning and integrating data from multiple sources.
* Exploring relationships between traffic and economic indicators.
* Identifying patterns through exploratory data analysis (EDA).
* Providing data-driven recommendations for transportation infrastructure investments.

---

## 📂 Data Sources

* **TomTom Traffic Index** – Urban mobility and congestion indicators.
* **OECD Cities Database** – Economic indicators, including GDP per capita.
